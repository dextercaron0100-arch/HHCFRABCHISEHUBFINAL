Franchise Hub was founded on vision, discipline, and a deep commitment to empowering Filipino entrepreneurs. Behind this vision are the Herrera Brothers - the driving force behind Herrera Holding Corporation and the strategic minds behind HHC Franchise Hub.

"Filipino enterprises deserve access to professional franchise systems, operational excellence, and scalable business frameworks."

With years of hands-on experience in building, operating, and scaling businesses across multiple industries, the Herrera Brothers understand that true growth is not achieved by expansion alone - but through structure, system, and sustainability.

Under their leadership, Franchise Hub was established not merely as a consultancy, but as a growth engine - a platform designed to transform promising local brands into well-structured, expansion-ready franchise systems and create meaningful, lasting legacies.

Chapter 01

Story

From bootstrapping the first kiosk to engineering a multi-format franchise portfolio, HHC was built on grit, supply-chain rigor, and a people-first training culture. Every playbook exists to shorten the distance between a dream and a profitable opening.

Chapter 02

Leadership

Our leadership philosophy combines boardroom-level governance with disciplined, on-the-ground execution. We integrate strategic oversight with real-time field intelligence - conducting store visits, evaluating operational performance, stress-testing product offerings, and rigorously validating unit economics before scaling. This balanced approach ensures that every expansion decision is grounded in data, operational reality, and long-term sustainability.

Governance Unit economics Ops discipline

Chapter 03

Future

At HHC Franchise Hub, our vision for the future is deliberate, innovation-driven, and anchored in long-term stewardship. We are building structured experience laboratories to continuously refine our concepts, implementing sustainability mandates that guide responsible expansion, and integrating AI-assisted rollout systems to enhance precision, speed, and scalability across our network. However, advancement for us is not defined by technology alone. True progress lies in balancing innovation with accountability - ensuring that every rollout strengthens operational excellence, protects brand integrity, and delivers measurable value to our partners. As we move forward, we remain firmly rooted in service, governance, and community impact. Our commitment is clear: to lead responsibly, scale intelligently, and build enterprises that endure for generations.
